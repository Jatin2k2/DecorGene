DecorGenie : Personalize AI Home Decor Assistant

Capstone Project ( CPG NO : 20 )

The DecorGenie project aims to address the challenge of providing personalized interior decor recommendations and visualization to users based on their uploaded room images.

Many individuals struggle with selecting the right decor styles and products that match their preferences and room aesthetics. Traditional methods of browsing through countless options on various e-commerce websites can be time-consuming and overwhelming.

DecorGenie seeks to leverage the power of machine learning and web scripting to automate and enhance this process, providing users with tailored decor suggestions and a seamless visualization experience.
